Company Profile

The origin of our company was first founded 50 years ago in Taiwan, Chang Hua province under the company name JIOU ZUN CO.,LTD. The plan of oversea expansion have led to the establishment of our Thailand company YOK SIN CO.,LTD in 1990.

Over 30 years of industry experience

During the past 30 years we have conducted various company restructuring, by adding more new machineries, increase technical standards and capacity, as well expanding our product range. Our clients includes Nike, Addidas, K-Swiss, Lacoste, various footwear, clothing, bags, underwear & lingerie manufacturers, as well as car parts accessories(seat belts, net pockets etc) and other manufacturers.
