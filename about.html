Company Profile
รายละเอียด บริษัท

YOK SIN CO., LTD is a well-established technical textile manufacturer with a distinguished history dating back to 1990. With over 30 years of expertise, we have established ourselves as a specialist in the production of a diverse range of fabric products, including premium garment accessories crafted from the finest quality yarns.

The origin of the company was first founded 50 years ago in Taiwan, Chang Hua province under the company name JIOU ZUN CO.,LTD. The plan of oversea expansion to Thailand have led to the establishment of our new headquarter company YOK SIN CO.,LTD in 1990.

Over 30 years of industry experience

Over the past 30 years, our company has undergone constant restructuring and growth. This includes the addition of advanced textile machines, improved technical standards and capacity, and a broadened product range. We also have gained extensive expertise in various textile sectors, such as footwear, apparel, automotive, and sporting textiles, including paragliding accessories.
